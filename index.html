Privacy Protection (The Details That Matter)

Look, privacy isn't just a marketing buzzword for us - it's how we built the entire system. Here's exactly what happens to your data:

Browser Processing

Your Excel file gets processed locally in your browser using WebAssembly technology. We extract only the cryptographic hash of the password - essentially a mathematical fingerprint that tells us nothing about your actual content.

Secure Transmission

That hash gets sent to our servers over encrypted connections. Our AI systems analyze it to determine the original password. Once found, we send back just the password - not your file, not your data, just those characters you forgot.

Auto-Delete

Everything gets automatically deleted within hours. No permanent storage, no data retention, no backup copies sitting on our servers.
